Welcome to Bitbucket
Let’s get started

Familiar with Bitbucket? Git back to it.

Repository management.
Stay on top of code changes and work in progress

Browse repositories within your organization’s projects and keep up to date with your team’s changes.
Quickly navigate through source, commits, branches and pull requests to find out what happened and when.

Flexible workflows.
Quality and speed without merge pain

With Git, your team can work on changes in branches and decide when and how they’re merged.

Bitbucket assists by guiding you from branch creation to feature merge.

For contractors, large teams and side-projects, forks provide an additional layer of separation and security.

Pull requests.
Get feedback right at the source

Bitbucket offers fast and focused code review using pull requests. They encourage teams to talk about their code.

Bitbucket diff view options help others understand your changes. See their comments inline and address feedback directly.

Link your changes back to Jira issues

View the full context of a code change and its associated issues with Jira and Bitbucket integration.

From within a pull request, you can view information on Jira issues, transition them, and resolve them without ever leaving Bitbucket.

If you’d like to learn more, check out our beginner’s guide to Git.
